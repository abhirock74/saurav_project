Wildlife Tours India - A Journey Into Indian Wildlife.


In the wildest and far off hinterlands, reside the entities of nature unique in their own right. Subtle, rare and single, they live a life of mystery for mankind. The diverse biodiversity of this land and the savaging details of its forests have bewildered people since long. Wildlife Explorer India takes pleasure in showcasing the grandeur of Indian Wildlife which is unmatched in its category. The land of the Royal Bengal Tigers, Asiatic Lions, Asian Elephants and the Greater One-Horned Rhinoceros, India has a unique flora and fauna which draws the wildlife lovers from all over the world.


Wildlife Explorer India has been operating wildlife safaris in India for the last 10 years and offer exclusive services in wildlife tourism through uniquely designed itineraries for wildlife tours in India. We have redefined wildlife tourism through our innovative ideas and experience in adventure tourism. All our wildlife safari trips in India are meticulously designed to provide you the best wilderness experience and encounters while ensure long term sustainability of our environment for our future travelers & generations. We focus on group and customized tours to suit every requirement. We have a dynamic team of wildlife enthusiasts who are experienced and accustomed to the ways of Indian wildlife. We believe in ecotourism and promote the practices of responsible tourism through all our tours and travels. We continuously strive to create unique travel experiences and memories for our customers. We also organize birding tours in India to some of the most known and unknown destinations across the country.


The founder of the company, Balvinder Singh (Bali), is himself a naturalist. A die hard lover of wildlife and photography, he has travelled extensively across the country to pursue his passion. He has been organizing whimsical wildlife tours in India for the last many years and has showcased the country with all its glitz and grandeur to his clients. He takes personal interest in wildlife tours right from planning to execution. Most of the featured tours have stemmed out of his personal travel experiences.


Uninhibited, earthy and outlandish, Indian wildlife calls for explorers to unearth the darkest secrets of its existence. Come and explore the rich diversity of Indian Wildlife.


Balvinder Singh (Bali): An expert to operate diverse Wildlife Tours in India


Founder of Wildlife Explorer India, is a wildlife enthusiast & photographer, having extensive experience leading wildlife & birding tours across India as Tour guide. He travels extensively in his quest to discover the wonders and wilderness of Indian wildlife and follows his passion for nature and photography. He has a wide range of experience from leading groups to supplying logistical support at the ground. His contacts, nurtured over many years, with hoteliers, local guides and safari drivers means exceptional service for his clients. All the photographs which you see on our website are from his own collection.


All our wildlife tours in India are planned to keep in mind the vulnerability of our environment and are available at competitive prices and the best of facilities. We also organize specialized Indian Wildlife photography tours and birding tours in India. As a responsible company, we also obey the rules laid by the Government and Forest department to promote Wildlife Tourism in India. We look forward to welcoming you in our land.
